Cleveland Tourism

Dining

The Hyatt Regency Cleveland At The Arcade is located near multiple restaurants accomodating a range of tastes and price points. Several dining venues can be found across Euclid Ave. which is easily accessable via the rear exit of the hotel.

Looking for a great coffee shop? Look no further than Rising Star Coffee Roasters. The shop is located inside the Hyatt Arcade and was named one of the ten best coffee shops in the country by USA Today.

Nearby Attractions

Within walking distance of Hyatt Regency Cleveland At The Arcade hotel, you will be able to find the following major attractions:

  • Tower City Center (Shopping Mall): 10 min walk (0.5 miles / 0.8 kilometers)
  • The Arcade Cleveland (Shopping mall): 4 min walk (0.2 miles / 0.3 kilometers)
  • Cleveland Public Square (Park): 4 min walk (0.2 miles / 0.3 kilometers)
  • Hungarian Heritage Museum (History museum): 7 min walk (0.4 miles / 0.6 kilometers)
  • Fountain of Eternal Life (Memorial park): 3 min walk (0.2 miles / 0.3 kilometers)
  • Great Lakes Science Center (Museum): 5 min walk (0.9 miles / 1.4 kilometers)
  • Rock & Roll Hall of Fame (Museum): 14 min walk (0.7 miles / 1.1 kilometers)
