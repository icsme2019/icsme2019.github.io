Traveling to Cleveland

General Climate Information

During the Fall season, you can expect temperatures to range between 55°F (13°C) to about 66°F (19°C).

Flying to Cleveland

The airport nearest to the conference venue is the Cleveland Hopkins International Airport (CLE).
This is approximatly a 20 minute drive to the venue depending on traffic.

Flights can also be obtained to the Akron-Canton Airport, but will take about a 55 minute drive depending on traffic to reach the hotel by car.

Getting to the Conference Venue

The conference venue is the Hyatt Regency Cleveland at the Arcade located at 420 Superior Ave, Cleveland, OH 44114. There are a variety of ways to get the the venue once you have arrive at the Cleveland Hopkins International Airport (CLE):

RTA Red Line from CLE to Downtown

The Cleveland Hopkins International Airport is directly connected to the RTA Red Line train service. It is located in the lower level of the airport (see map). Departures from the airport occur every 15 minutes and only costs $2.50 per ride. The closest stop to the conference venue is the Tower City-Public Square Station which should take between 20 and 30 minutes. From there, it is about a 10 minute walk to the conference venue at the Hyatt Regency Cleveland at the Arcade. Additional details about the RTA Red Line service can be found on the Cleveland Hopkins Airport website and the RTA website.

Taxi from CLE to the Conference

Taxis are available from the airport and a ride to the venue should cost between $45 and $55 (depending on traffic) including tip and take around 20-30 minutes.

Getting around in Cleveland

If you want to travel further than walking distance you can use:

  • Ride Sharing Services (Uber or Lyft)
  • Taxi Services
  • Public Transportation
