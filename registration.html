For Authors

  • Each accepted paper must have at least one author registered at a professional member rate. Papers for which the only registered authors are students must have one of the students register at the full (i.e., non-student) rate. Note that this policy does not apply to doctoral symposium papers.
  • One author registration is enough, even if presenting several, co-authored papers.

Invitation letter for visa

First the prospective participants need to pay the registration fee, then you can issue an invitation letter (therefore, please contact jmaletic@kent.edu) and only then they can apply for a visa.

Students

You must certify your student status by means of some academic document (for example, a copy of your university fees pay-slip) or a letter from your thesis supervisor, departmental or institutional director, or dean of faculty.
