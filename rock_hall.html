Rock & Roll Hall of Fame

One of our ICSME social events will take place at the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame on Thursday, October 3rd at 19:00.

This event will feature dining and museum access for attendees.

About the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame

Located on the scenic shore of Lake Erie, the Cleveland Rock & Roll Hall of Fame is a stunning architectural landmark featuring multiple floors of exhibits and interactive experiences.

Attendees of the conference will have the opportunity to both dine and explore the largest collection of Rock memorabilia in the world.

Directions to the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame

  • Exit the hotel venue from the front entrace facing Superior Ave.
  • Turn right and head northeast on Superior Ave.
  • Turn left and head north on East 9th St. for 0.5 miles (0.8 km)
  • Turn left on Erie Side Ave. and you should be able to see the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame
